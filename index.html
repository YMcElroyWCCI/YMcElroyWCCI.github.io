About Me

Yuli McElroy professional life has been in business but her love remains in coding.

Graduated with a Master Degree in Accounting and Financial Management from Keller Graduate School of Management along with graduate certificate in Information System Management. Her love of coding began in 2001, created an online export import small business using XML markup language. After many twists and turns in life, the coding sparks returned during school project while creating a simple calculator using Visual Basic.Net programming language. She continuously enhances her DevOps knowledge and experience focusing on agile Full-Stack software development using IntelliJ and Eclipse IDEA through various hands-on projects, both independent and collaborative setting, at We Can Code IT, an intensive coding bootcamp based in Columbus, OH.

Over 15 years of experience in Accounting, Finance, and Business Management in Global Financial Industries. A self-starter, a team player, and a problem-solver. She would love to combine her business and technology skills to create things that make a difference with one code at a time.
